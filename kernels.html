Deep intuition

A kernel is a template matcher.

Positive weights say "I want bright pixels here." Negative weights say "I want darker pixels here." When the local patch matches that pattern, the products add to a large response.

Vertical line kernel

Rewards a bright center column and punishes bright side columns.

Horizontal line kernel

Rewards a bright center row and punishes bright rows above and below.

Edge kernels

Respond to change from dark to bright, so sign tells direction.

CNN connection

A convolutional layer learns many such kernels instead of hand-picking them.
