What gets summed

Deep intuition

You are not averaging outcomes. You are summing explanations.

The output z can happen in several mutually exclusive ways. The pair grid lists those ways. Because they are different hidden explanations of the same visible sum, their probabilities add.

Why multiply?

If X and Y are independent, one pair has probability P(X=x) times P(Y=y).

Why add?

The valid pairs are alternative ways to get the same z.

Why diagonals?

Every cell on one diagonal has the same x+y value.

Shape story

Middle sums are often bigger because more pair splits reach them.
