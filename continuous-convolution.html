Deep intuition

Continuous convolution is the same hidden-split idea.

There are infinitely many possible splits x and z-x. A single split has no probability by itself, so the calculation accumulates density over a small continuum of neighboring splits.

Exact z has density

The curve height is not P(Z=z). Probability lives in intervals.

Overlap means compatibility

Large overlap means many plausible splits support that z.

Uniform plus uniform

The overlap length grows, then shrinks, creating a triangle.

Normal plus normal

The result stays normal because independent noise variances add.
