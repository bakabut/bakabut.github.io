What the network learns

The model is not trained to invent an image in one jump.

During training, a clean example is mixed with a known amount of random noise. The network sees the noisy example and the timestep, then learns to predict the noise that was added. At sampling time, that prediction becomes a direction for taking one cleaner step.

Forward process

Known math adds Gaussian noise until the data becomes almost pure noise.

Denoiser

A neural net learns local cleanup directions for every noise level.

Sampler

Many small denoising steps turn random noise into a plausible sample.

Conditioning

Text, labels, or images can bias the denoising direction toward a desired result.
